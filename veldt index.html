How It's Made
01

Source

We travel to the origin. Mongolian highlands for cashmere, Tuscan tanneries for leather. Nothing is anonymous.

02

Design

Each piece begins as negative space — what to remove, not what to add. Reduction is our language.

03

Craft

Hand-sewn in our Antwerp atelier. A single coat takes 40 hours. We never count them.

04

Deliver

Wrapped in unbleached linen, sealed with wax. The unboxing is the first wear.
